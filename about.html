Our Story

La Vie Nail Lounge was founded with a simple mission: to provide exceptional nail and beauty services in a welcoming, luxurious environment. We believe every client deserves to feel pampered and confident. Our team of licensed technicians continually updates their skills and techniques to offer you the latest trends while maintaining the highest standards of hygiene and safety.

Hygiene & Safety

We take sanitation seriously. All instruments are sterilised using hospital‑grade autoclaves and sealed for your protection. Files and buffers are single‑use and disposed of after each client. Our pedicure chairs are cleaned and disinfected between appointments. Your health and peace of mind are our top priority.

Meet the Team

Our talented technicians come from diverse backgrounds but share a passion for beauty and customer care. Each member of our team is certified in their specialty, whether that’s Gel‑X, acrylic, nail art, lash extensions or skincare. We foster a culture of continuous education to ensure you receive the best service possible.
