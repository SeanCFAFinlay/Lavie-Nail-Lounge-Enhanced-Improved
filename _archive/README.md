# Archived pages — superseded by the 2026 rebuild

These files are the previous version of the site. They are kept for reference only
and are **not linked from the live site**. Nothing here should be republished as-is.

They were moved out of the site root because they contain content that could not be
verified against the salon's own sources, and in several cases directly contradicts them:

- **Invented pricing.** `services.html` and `index.html` advertised "from $80" for Gel‑X,
  "from $70" for extensions, "from $50" for pedicures and so on. The salon's actual Dash
  Booking menu prices Gel‑X at **$75**, extensions from **$60**, and pedicure services from
  **$25**. None of the old "from" prices matched.
- **Invented promotions.** "15% off your first Gel‑X new set", "complimentary paraffin
  treatment", "Lash & Brow Combo — save 20%". No such offers appear on lavienaillounge.ca
  or in the booking system.
- **Invented staff.** `team.html` listed seventeen technicians with biographies,
  specialities, years of experience ("over 10 years") and weekly availability tables.
  None of this is published by the salon.
- **Invented schedules.** `services.html` carried three "technician schedule" tables that
  were labelled a mockup but presented as real availability.
- **Unverified claims.** `about.html` asserted hospital‑grade autoclave sterilisation,
  single‑use files and buffers, and licensed/certified technicians. These may well be true,
  but they are not published by the salon and were not confirmed.
- **An unconfirmed third location.** `locations.html` listed a Langley studio at
  19855 88 Ave #107 with phone 778‑726‑0888. The live site instead lists
  3D Nails Spa at 8850 Walnut Grove Dr, Langley Twp, phone 604‑888‑1619.

If any of the above turns out to be accurate, it can be reinstated — but it needs to come
from the salon directly rather than from these files.
